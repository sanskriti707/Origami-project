Intresting facts about camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

chameleon

Intersting facts about chameleon

  • Almost half of the population of chameleon live in madagascar.
  • Contrary to popular belief, when a chameleon changes its skin color, the animal usually isn’t trying to camouflage itself by blending into the environment. More often, this remarkable ability is used as a way of controlling its body temperature.
  • Chameleon's each eye has the incredible range of motion of 90 degrees vertically and 180 degrees horizontally.

Intersting facts about pigeon

  • Their navigational skills are so strong that they can find their nest from 1300 miles away.
  • In a 2016 study, scientists showed that pigeons can differentiate between strings of letters and actual words.
  • According to one study, they're more efficient multitaskers than people are.
